Query: future of ai agents in 2026

Google Cloud’s 2026 AI Agent Trends Report highlights that AI agents will significantly transform business operations across sectors by enabling productivity gains, agentic workflows, hyper-personalized customer experiences, enhanced security, and workforce training [1]. Key examples include Telus employees saving 40 minutes per AI interaction and Suzano reducing query time by 95% using Gemini Pro agents, while Salesforce and Google Cloud are building cross-platform agents via the Agent2Agent protocol to automate complex workflows [1]. These agents will move beyond chatbots to deliver proactive, context-aware services, such as Danfoss automating 80% of email-based order processing and reducing customer response times from 42 hours to near real time, and Macquarie Bank improving fraud protection with 38% more self-service adoption [1].
